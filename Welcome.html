F.A.Q

 What'd be the goal ROI for my ads?

In terms of ROI, there have been several miconceptions even among marketers ourselves!

In our case we employ Gross Margin ROI as your KPI threshold, which is calculated in:

(Sales * Gross Margin - Marketing Cost) / Marketing Cost

along with metrics like ROAS = Revenue dollar / Ads dollar Spent.

Why use 2 sets of metrics? For your instance, a 10 - 20 ROAS at first glacne may look remarkable, but for clients coming from low margin industries this could end up at sub 0% ROI, meaning it's run on negative cash flow basis!

Since we do care that you achieve your goal without hurting yourself, we would enforce a minimal gross ROI of 0% so you could at least enjoy some expossures from a SAFE HOUSE.

Just don't advertize for the sake of advertizing! Have the goal set before spending a cent!

 What if my sales doesn't go well?

First of all, with a solid product or service in place, it'd not be that hard to get sales, especially with a team of marketing pros backing you up.

But in case your sales really does underperforms, we can wait up to 2x the average time, or roughly 5 years for you to hit the cap.

 What if I go out of business?

In such case you're obligated to pay back the unpaid cost, A.K.A the other half of the total cost.

We don't count in the upside 0.25x since it apparently would not ve pracital in events like this.
